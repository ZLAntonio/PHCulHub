Zee Antonio

Meet Zee. She brings a unique perspective shaped by her immigration journey from the Philippines at the age of 16. Although now residing in the United States, her heart remains closely tied to her roots in the Pearl of the Orient Seas. During her time in the Philippines, Zee cherished shopping at wet markets with her grandmother, exploring the diverse islands, and experiencing the unique shopping centers. These experiences have deeply influenced her cultural perspective. Zee's love for Filipino culture and cuisine is evident, with a special fondness for Bicol Express and the seasonal delight, Puto Bumbong.
